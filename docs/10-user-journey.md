## 4. Person arrives from outside

**Goal:** Not get lost when arriving via direct link.

**Path:**

- Arrives from Google or link
- → Reads content
- → Sees menu and footer
- → Navigates to Home, {{SECTION}} or {{ACTION}}

No page is a dead end.

---

## 5. Validation rule

**A journey is correct if:**

1. The person can understand {{CORE_CONCEPT}} in fewer than three clicks.
2. There is always a "next" or "back".
3. They never land on a dead screen.
4. They never feel they entered an app or store.

---

## 6. Journey accessibility

The journey must be possible for anyone, regardless of physical, sensory or cognitive abilities.

Accessibility does not define alternate paths. It ensures the same journey can be traversed by:

- People with visual disabilities
- People with low vision
- People who navigate with keyboard
- People with hearing difficulties
- People with high cognitive load

Technical and editorial criteria in `19-accessibility-standards`.
